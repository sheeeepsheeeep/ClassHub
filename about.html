About ClassHub

ClassHub is a smart classroom booking system designed specifically for university students and staff. Our platform streamlines the process of reserving campus classrooms, ensuring efficient use of academic spaces and improving overall campus organization.

Our Mission

To make classroom management seamless by providing a convenient, reliable, and transparent booking system for students and educators.

Why ClassHub?

Managing room availability manually is time-consuming. ClassHub automates the booking process, reduces scheduling conflicts, and ensures students can find suitable learning spaces with ease.

Our Values

Efficiency, accessibility, user-centered design, and smart campus solutions. We believe technology should simplify daily academic life.

Our Services

Real-Time Availability

Check which classrooms are open for booking instantly

Easy Booking & Confirmation

Reserve classrooms quickly and receive instant confirmation

Administrator Control

Manage, approve, or deny reservations to ensure fair usage

Flexible Scheduling

Book rooms for lectures, meetings, study sessions, and events

Optimized Utilization

Reduce scheduling conflicts and maximize space usage

Secure & Restricted Access

Only ClassHub members can make classroom reservations
